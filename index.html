Welcome to OYOVO

OYOVO'S WORK

At One Youth One Voice, we believe in the power of young people to drive change. Through leadership training, mentorship, and skills development programs, we empower youth to become active citizens, entrepreneurs, and leaders in their communities.

Join us in unlocking the potential of Malawi's youth.

Learn more about our programs

AIM

To empower and unite the youth of Malawi in advocating for social, economic, and political change, fostering a culture of active citizenship and engagement that promotes sustainable development and equity.

See more

OUR GOALS

  • Youth Empowerment: Equip young people with essential skills and knowledge through training programs, workshops, and mentorship opportunities that foster leadership, entrepreneurship, and civic engagement.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: Raise awareness on critical issues affecting youth in Malawi, such as education, health, employment, and climate change, by organizing campaigns and community outreach programs.
  • Policy Influence: Actively engage with policymakers and stakeholders to ensure youth perspectives are included in decision-making processes, particularly in areas that impact their futures.
  • Community Development: Facilitate community-driven projects that address local challenges, promoting youth participation in initiatives that enhance social cohesion and economic growth.
  • Networking and Collaboration: Build a strong network of youth organizations and groups across Malawi to foster collaboration, share resources, and amplify the collective voice for change.
  • Research and Innovation: Conduct research to identify the needs and challenges of youth in Malawi, using findings to inform programs and influence policy, while promoting innovative solutions to local problems.
  • Mental Health and Well-being: Promote mental health awareness and provide support systems for youth, addressing stigma and ensuring access to resources that contribute to overall well-being.
  • Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs): Align organizational initiatives with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, empowering youth to contribute to global efforts for sustainable development and social justice.

Objectives

Empowerment through Leadership Training
  • Develop Leadership Skills: Conduct workshops to enhance leadership competencies among youth.
  • Mentorship Programs: Pair young leaders with experienced mentors in their fields of interest.
  • Public Speaking Workshops: Offer training to improve communication and public speaking skills.
  • Youth Councils: Establish youth councils to encourage civic engagement and decision-making.
  • Conflict Resolution Training: Teach youth strategies for resolving conflicts peacefully.
Mental Health Awareness and Support
  • Mental Health Workshops: Organize workshops to promote mental well-being and awareness.
  • Peer Support Networks: Create peer support groups for sharing experiences and coping strategies.
  • Stress Management Programs: Provide resources and training on stress management techniques.
  • Access to Counseling Services: Facilitate access to professional mental health resources for youth.
  • Mindfulness and Well-being Initiatives: Introduce mindfulness practices to improve emotional resilience.
Charity Activities for Vulnerable Children and Youth
  • Fundraising Events: Organize events to raise funds for vulnerable children’s education and health.
  • Volunteer Programs: Encourage youth to volunteer in shelters or community centers.
  • School Supplies Drives: Collect and distribute school supplies to underprivileged children.
  • Mentorship for Vulnerable Youth: Implement mentorship programs targeting at-risk youth.
  • Health Camps: Organize health camps to provide free medical check-ups for vulnerable populations.
Community Engagement and Social Responsibility
  • Community Service Projects: Initiate community service projects that address local needs.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Launch campaigns on social issues affecting youth, such as bullying and substance abuse.
  • Civic Education Programs: Educate youth on their rights and responsibilities as citizens.
  • Sustainability Initiatives: Promote environmental awareness through community clean-up and tree-planting events.
  • Cultural Exchange Programs: Facilitate cultural exchange initiatives to promote diversity and inclusion.
Support for the Elderly
  • Elder Care Volunteer Programs: Encourage youth to volunteer in nursing homes or senior centers.
  • Intergenerational Programs: Create initiatives that facilitate interactions between youth and the elderly to share knowledge and experiences.
  • Health and Wellness Workshops: Organize workshops focused on physical and mental health for elderly individuals.
  • Technology Training: Teach elderly individuals how to use technology to stay connected with family and friends.
  • Advocacy for Elder Rights: Empower youth to advocate for the rights and needs of the elderly in their communities.
Empowering Young Political Leaders
  • Political Engagement Programs: Organize training sessions on political processes and civic engagement for young leaders.
  • Youth Political Forums: Create platforms for young political leaders to discuss and debate relevant issues.
  • Policy Development Workshops: Equip youth with skills to develop and propose policies that impact their communities.
  • Collaboration with Local Government: Facilitate partnerships between youth and local government to address community issues.
  • Leadership in Social Movements: Support youth-led social movements by providing training and resources on advocacy.

By providing training, resources, and modern farming techniques, we aim to boost productivity, enhance food security, and create sustainable economic opportunities for young farmers across Malawi.
